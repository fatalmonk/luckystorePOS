## 1. Brand Story

### Who We Are
Lucky Store is your neighborhood family grocer in Chattogram — not a faceless supermarket, not a premium boutique. We bridge the gap between the traditional corner store and modern convenience: **fresh produce, daily essentials, same-day delivery**, with the warmth and familiarity of a shop that remembers your name.

### Our Promise
> *"Fresh from the market to your kitchen. Same day, every day."*

### Positioning Statement
For busy families in Chattogram who want quality groceries without the hassle of crowded markets, Lucky Store is the neighborhood grocer that combines traditional freshness with modern convenience — because your family deserves food that feels like home.

### Brand Pillars

| Pillar | What it means | How we sound |
|--------|---------------|--------------|
| **Fresh** | Sourced daily, never stale, seasonal | "ভোরের সবজি এখন আপনার দরজায়" (Morning vegetables, now at your doorstep) |
| **Trust** | Transparent pricing, honest stock counts | "আমরা যা দেখাই, তাই পাবেন" (What you see is what you get) |
| **Warm** | Friendly, familial, never corporate | "আপনার পরিবারের জন্য, আমাদের পরিবার থেকে" (For your family, from our family) |
| **Easy** | Simple ordering, cash on delivery, no apps needed | "বাড়ি বসে অর্ডার করুন, ক্যাশ অন ডেলিভারি" (Order from home, pay on delivery) |

## 2. Logo

### Concept Direction
Two approved logo directions are being explored. Until final selection:

**Option A — Leaf Bag Mark**
A stylized shopping bag with a fresh green leaf growing from it, integrated with a bold "L". Symbolizes growth, freshness, and delivery.

**Option B — Star Crest Badge**
A circular crest with a 4-point star at center, surrounded by a wheat/grain wreath. "LUCKY" arcs the top, "STORE" the bottom. Premium but approachable.

### Logo Files
- Primary: Full color on light backgrounds
- Inverse: White on dark/accent backgrounds
- Icon only: "L" mark for favicons, app icons, small spaces

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "L" in all directions:

```
   ┌─────────────┐
   │   [logo]    │
   │             │
   └─────────────┘
      ↑←──L──→↑
```

### Minimum Sizes

| Application | Width |
|-------------|-------|
| Website header | `120px` minimum |
| Mobile app icon | `48×48dp` minimum |
| Social avatar | `400×400px` recommended |
| Print (flyers, bags) | `20mm` minimum |
| Receipt header | `15mm` minimum |

### Incorrect Usage
❌ Don't stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
❌ Don't change the colors arbitrarily
❌ Don't place on busy photographic backgrounds without a safe zone
❌ Don't recreate or redraw — always use approved files

|-------|------|--------|-------------|----------------|
| Hero H1 | `clamp(1.5rem, 3vw, 2.25rem)` | 800 | 1.2 | `-0.02em` |

| Button | `0.875rem` | 600 | 1 | normal |

### Mixed-Language Typography Rules
- English headlines, body, prices: Inter
- Bangla headlines: Hind Siliguri Bold (`700`)
- Bangla body: Hind Siliguri Regular (`400`)
- Never mix serif + sans-serif on the same line
- Never use Comic Sans, Poppins, or decorative display fonts

## 5. Photography Style

### Aesthetic: Warm Kitchen Lifestyle

Our photography should feel like a **Saturday morning in a Bangladeshi home kitchen** — warm, lived-in, appetizing, never sterile.

### Do's
✅ Shoot in natural daylight (golden hour preferred)
✅ Use warm wood, terracotta, or concrete textures as backgrounds
✅ Include contextual props: betel leaf, clay bowls, steel glasses, checkered kitchen towels
✅ Show real imperfect produce: curved gourds, soil on root vegetables
✅ Capture steam, droplets, flour dust — sensory details
✅ Mix close-up detail shots with wider kitchen/lifestyle compositions

### Don'ts
❌ White seamless studio shots (too sterile)
❌ Overly styled Pinterest-aesthetic (unreachable)
❌ Cold fluorescent lighting
❌ Plastic packaging as hero (we sell unpackaged fresh goods)

### Composition Guidelines
- Rule of thirds for product hero shots
- Shallow depth of field (blur backgrounds)
- 45-degree angle for tabletop food shots
- Eye-level for market/vendor storytelling shots

### Aspect Ratios by Channel

| Channel | Ratio | Recommended |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| Instagram Feed | `1:1` or `4:5` | `1080×1350px` |
| Instagram Story/Reel | `9:16` | `1080×1920px` |
| Facebook Post | `1:1` | `1200×1200px` |
| Facebook Cover | `16:9` | `820×312px` |
| WhatsApp Status | `9:16` | `1080×1920px` |
| Website Hero | `16:9` or `3:2` | `1440×960px` |
| Flyer | `A5` or `A4` portrait | `148×210mm` or `210×297mm` |
| Signage | `1:2` or `2:3` | Vector, scalable |

## 6. Social Media

### Instagram

#### Feed Posts
- **Primary template:** Lifestyle photo + text overlay bottom-left
- **Overlay text:** Bold white or `#1c1917` with subtle drop-shadow
- **Caption style:** 2–3 lines Bangla hook, then English details, then hashtags
- **Hashtag strategy:** `#LuckyStoreBD #ChattogramGrocery #SameDayDelivery #FreshProduce #LocalMarket`

**Example caption:**
```
আজকের ভোরের পালং শাক — সরাসরি কৃষকের ঘর থেকে আপনার কিচেনে।
Morning's fresh spinach — straight from the farmer to your kitchen.

Order by 11 AM for same-day delivery 🛵
📍 Emdad Park, Chattogram

#LuckyStoreBD #FreshChattogram #SameDayDelivery
```

#### Stories
- **Background:** Product photo or warm gradient (`#faf8f5` → `#f0eee6`)
- **Top sticker:** Price in large bold text
- **CTA sticker:** "Swipe up" or "Tap to order" in terracotta
- **Font:** Inter Bold for English, Hind Siliguri Bold for Bangla

#### Reels
- **Format:** 15–30 seconds
- **Content:** Unpacking fresh delivery, time-lapse of market sourcing, quick recipe
- **Audio:** Trending Bangla folk or acoustic — no corporate muzak

### Facebook

#### Post Structure
1. **Hook line:** Question or bold statement (Bangla preferred)
2. **Body:** Details in mixed code-switching
3. **CTA:** "অর্ডার করতে মেসেজ করুন" or "DM to order"
4. **Link:** `luckystorebd.com` or WhatsApp direct link

#### Cover Photo
- Update seasonally: Eid specials, Ramadan iftar bundles, winter vegetable harvest
- Always include logo top-left, tagline bottom-right

### WhatsApp

#### Business Profile
- **Business Name:** Lucky Store
- **Category:** Grocery Store
- **Description:** "Your neighborhood grocer in Emdad Park, Chattogram. Fresh produce, daily essentials, same-day delivery. Cash on delivery."
- **Hours:** Daily 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- **Catalog:** Auto-sync from inventory DB

#### Broadcast Templates

- **Format:** 9:16 image with overlaid price + "Swipe up to order"
- **Content:** Daily arrivals, behind-the-scenes, customer testimonials

## 7. Tone of Voice

### Personality Traits

| Trait | How it shows up | Example |
|-------|-----------------|---------|
| **Neighborly** | Use "tumi" (তুমি) or "apni" (আপনি), not corporate "valued customer" | "আপনার অর্ডার প্রস্তুত হচ্ছে" |
| **Helpful** | Anticipate questions, offer alternatives | "টমেটো শেষ? ফেসবুকে জানান — আমরা জোগাড় করে দেব" |
| **Honest** | Admit when out of stock, suggest substitutes | "দুঃখিত, মুরগির মাংস আজ শেষ। বিফ চাইলে ৳৫২০/কেজি" |
| **Warm** | Use seasonal greetings, celebrate with customers | "ঈদ মোবারক! স্পেশাল কুরবানি বান্ডেল দেখুন" |
| **Simple** | One idea per message, short sentences | No long paragraphs. Break into bullets. |

### Language Mixing Rules

| Context | Primary | Secondary |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| WhatsApp chat | Bangla | English for product names, prices |
| WhatsApp broadcast | Bangla hook | English details |
| Instagram/Facebook caption | Bangla | English for hashtags |
| Push notifications | Bangla | English product names |
| Website headings | English | Bangla subtitle optional |
| Receipts | Bangla | English item names |
| Print flyers | Mixed, leader line in Bangla | — |

### Words We Use / Avoid

| ✅ Use | ❌ Avoid |
|--------|----------|
| "ভোরের তাজা" (morning fresh) | "premium organic artisan" |
| "দরজায় পৌঁছে দেওয়া" (doorstep delivery) | "logistics solution" |
| "টাকা" / "৳" | "$" or "USD" |
| "শেষ হয়ে গেছে" (sold out) | "out of stock" (too cold) |
| "আবার আসবে" (coming back) | "restock ETA unknown" |
| "বাড়ি বসে" (from home) | "e-commerce platform" |

## 8. Print Collateral

### Flyers (A5, double-sided)

**Front:**
- Top: Logo + "Lucky Store — Emdad Park"
- Middle: Hero lifestyle photo of fresh produce spread
- Overlay: "একই দিনে ডেলিভারি | Same-Day Delivery"
- Bottom: WhatsApp QR code + phone number

**Back:**
- Top: "This week's fresh picks" with 6 featured products, prices, emojis
- Middle: Store hours + address
- Bottom: "Scan to order online" with website QR

### Signage

**Storefront Sign**
- Material: Acrylic or wood-effect vinyl on aluminum
- Colors: Terracotta logo on ivory background, or inverse on dark panel
- Text: "Lucky Store" in Inter Bold, Bengali tagline "তাজা ও বিশ্বস্ত" below
- Illumination: Warm white LED backlighting

**Window Clings**
- "Cash on Delivery ✓"
- "Same-Day Delivery 🛵"
- "Fresh Every Morning 🌿"
- Format: Individual circles, `30cm` diameter

### Delivery Packaging

**Paper Bags**
- Material: Brown kraft, recycled
- Print: Logo terracotta on one side, tagline + address below
- Size: Small (`20×25×10cm`), Medium (`30×35×15cm`), Large (`40×45×20cm`)

**Receipts**
- Thermal print, `80mm` width
- Header: Logo (small) + "Lucky Store" + date/time
- Body: Itemized list with Bangla + English, qty, unit price, line total
- Footer: "ধন্যবাদ! | Thank you!" + WhatsApp number + "See you tomorrow!"

## 10. Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do
- Lead with empathy — we're a neighbor, not a corporation
- Use customer's name when known (WhatsApp, notifications)
- Show real imperfections in photography
- Celebrate local Chattogram — reference neighborhoods, weather, festivals
- Respond to messages within 15 minutes during business hours
- Update social/WhatsApp status at least twice daily
- Honor the promise: if we say "same-day," it means same-day

### ❌ Don't
- Use generic stock photos of Western supermarkets
- Copy-paste corporate speak from big e-commerce sites
- Ignore out-of-stock items — always suggest alternatives
- Send broadcast messages after 9 PM
- Use more than 2 font weights in a single composition
- Place logo on backgrounds darker than `#30302e` without inverse version
