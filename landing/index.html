Installation Instructions

For Android:

  1. Click the download button above
  2. On GitHub Releases page, click "app-release.apk" under Assets
  3. Once downloaded, open your phone's Settings
  4. Go to Security or Privacy
  5. Enable "Unknown sources" or "Install unknown apps"
  6. Find the downloaded APK and tap to install
  7. Open the app and start managing your store!
Note: This app is not yet available on Google Play Store. You are downloading directly from our official GitHub releases.
